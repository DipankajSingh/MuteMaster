Privacy Policy

App Name: MuteMaster - Auto Silent Manager

Developer Contact: support@mutemasterapp.com

Effective Date: January 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MuteMaster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information. By using the MuteMaster app, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection & Permissions

We believe in data minimization. We only request permissions and collect data that is strictly necessary for the app's core functionality.

A. Location Data (Background & Foreground)

DISCLOSURE: The MuteMaster collects location data to enable the 'Automatic Mute' feature, which silences your device when you enter specific zones (like your office or school), even when the app is closed or not in use.
  • Data Collected: Precise coordinates (Latitude/Longitude).
  • Necessity: Required to define Geofence boundaries and detect entry/exit events.

B. Notification Permissions

  • Data Collected: Permission status to post notifications.
  • Necessity: To alert you when MuteMaster has automatically changed your ringer mode (e.g., "Muted phone at Office"). This ensures you are always aware of your phone's status.

C. Device State (Do Not Disturb Access)

  • Data Collected: Current ringer mode status.
  • Necessity: Required to actually change your phone from "Ring" to "Vibrate" or "Silent".

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the services within the app. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or user profiling.

  • Geofencing: Location data is processed by the Google Play Services Geofencing API to determine if you are inside a designated zone.
  • Automation: Device state data is used to trigger the volume change logic.
  • Communication: Notification data is used to send local system alerts regarding app activity.

3. Data Storage & Retention Practices

We adhere to a "Local-Only" architecture.

  • Storage: All user-defined zones, location logs, and settings are stored locally on your device's internal storage using secure databases (SQLite) and Shared Preferences.
  • No Cloud Uploads: We do not operate a backend server. Your location history is never transmitted to us or any cloud storage provider.
  • Retention Period: Data is retained only as long as the application is installed on your device.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the application acts as a hard delete. All data associated with MuteMaster is immediately removed from the device storage by the Android Operating System upon uninstallation.

4. User Rights and Choices

You maintain full control over your data at all times.

  • Right to Opt-Out: You may deny or revoke permissions (Location, Notifications, DND Access) at any time via your device settings. Note: Revoking location permissions will disable the automatic mute feature.
  • Right to Access & Edit: You can view and modify all saved locations directly within the app interface.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete individual zones within the app, or uninstall the app to permanently erase all data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to support the app's infrastructure. These services may collect information identified in their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services: Used for the Geofencing API and Maps display. Google Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

This service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for immediate removal.
