Privacy Policy

App Name: MuteMaster - Auto Silent Manager

Developer Contact: support@mutemasterapp.com

Effective Date: January 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the MuteMaster app (hereby referred to as "Application") and governs data collection and usage. By using the Application, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

1. Prominent Disclosure: Location Access

To provide the core functionality of the Application, we require specific access to your location data.

The MuteMaster collects location data to enable the "Automatic Mute" feature, which silences your device when you enter specific zones (like your office or school), even when the app is closed or not in use.

If you decline this permission, the automatic geofencing features will not function.

2. Data Collection: What, Why, and When

We adhere to the principle of data minimization. We only collect data necessary for the app to function.

A. Precise Location Data

  • What: GPS coordinates (Latitude/Longitude).
  • Why: To detect when your device enters or exits the "Silent Zones" you have created.
  • When: The Android OS monitors your location in the background. The app receives a trigger only when a geofence transition occurs.

B. Notification Permissions (Android 13+)

  • What: Permission to post status bar alerts.
  • Why: To inform you when the app has automatically changed your ringer mode (e.g., "Phone Muted").
  • When: Sent immediately after a zone entry/exit event.

C. Device Settings (Do Not Disturb)

  • What: Read/Write access to Audio settings.
  • Why: To toggle the phone between Ring, Vibrate, and Silent modes.
  • When: Accessed only when a zone transition is detected.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage Policy: All user-generated data (saved zones, location logs, preferences) is stored locally on your device using internal databases (SQLite).

Retention Period: Data is retained only for the duration of your use of the Application. We do not have access to your data.

Deletion: Uninstalling the Application acts as a permanent deletion of all data associated with the Application from your device.

4. Government & Regional Compliance Rules

If you are a resident of the EEA or UK, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on Consent (when you grant permissions) and Contractual Necessity (to provide the service you installed the app for).

  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently erases all personal data.
