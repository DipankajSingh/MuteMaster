# Privacy Policy - TheMuteMaster

**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction

TheMuteMaster ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise handles information about you.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Information
- **Precise Location (Fine Location)**: We collect your precise GPS location to determine when you are within a muted zone.
- **Approximate Location (Coarse Location)**: We use network-based location data (cell towers, Wi-Fi networks) for zone detection.
- **Background Location**: We collect location data while the app runs in the background to automatically mute/unmute your phone even when the app is not actively open.

**Purpose**: This location data is essential to the core functionality of TheMuteMaster—automatically detecting when you enter or exit designated muted zones.

### 2.2 Device Information
- Device manufacturer, model, and OS version
- App usage statistics and crash reports
- Boot completion status (for app initialization after device restart)

### 2.3 Notification Information
- Notification policy status
- Notification settings and preferences you configure within the app

### 2.4 User-Configured Data
- Muted zone locations and settings (stored locally on your device)
- Custom zone names and descriptions
- Preferred mute profiles

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide Core Functionality**: Detect your location and automatically mute/unmute your device
- **Improve Services**: Analyze app performance and fix bugs
- **Device Compatibility**: Ensure the app works correctly on your device after boot
- **User Experience**: Remember your zone preferences and settings
- **Customer Support**: Help resolve issues you report

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- **Location Data**: Muted zones and location history are stored locally on your device
- **User Preferences**: All your settings remain on your device
- **No Cloud Backup**: By default, your location data is not transmitted to external servers

### 4.2 Security Measures
- We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data
- Location data remains on your device unless you explicitly enable backup features
- Access to sensitive permissions requires explicit user consent

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 What We Do NOT Share
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your location data to third parties
- We do not share your muted zones with other applications without your permission
- Your location history is kept private

### 5.2 When We May Share
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or valid legal process
- **Safety**: We may share information to prevent harm or protect our services
- **Service Providers**: Only with trusted partners who help us operate the app (with strict confidentiality agreements)

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Permission Control
- You can grant or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
- You can disable background location tracking
- You can remove or modify muted zones at any time

### 6.2 Data Access and Deletion
- All your muted zone data is stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app
- You can export your settings or request a copy of your data

### 6.3 Opt-Out Options
- Disable location services to stop automatic mute/unmute functionality
- Turn off background app refresh to prevent background location collection
- You can uninstall the app at any time

## 7. Children's Privacy

TheMuteMaster is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Location Data**: Retained locally until you delete the app or manually clear app data
- **User Settings**: Retained as long as you use the app
- **Device Restart Data**: Boot completion status is temporary and not retained

## 9. Third-Party Services

TheMuteMaster may use third-party libraries and services for:
- Analytics and crash reporting
- Location services APIs provided by Android
- Push notifications

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our GitHub Pages and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
